About Us

Roger Morrison Excavating, LLC is a family owned excavating business owned by Roger Morrison, who has worked in the industry for over 40 years. We provide equipment, labor and expertise for various excavation jobs. The company has served Wayne, Ontario and Monroe County since its inception. We primarily target residential work and subcontract with many reputable home builders.
